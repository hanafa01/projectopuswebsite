FAQ

Recent Questions:

What is the projectopus?

The Projectopus is a platform that provides mutual benefit for both issuers and investors by providing issuers the opportunity to finance their projects, and promising investors high ROI.

What is Provate & Probid?

Provate is the Issuers platform, while Probid is the Adopters platform.

What is the value proposition?
Issuer:
  • Optimizing and providing a strategic roadmap
  • Quick and effortless exposure to potential markets globally
  • Penetration of new markets
Investor:
  • Access to alternative assets
  • Access to unique and limited professionally-filtered innovative concepts from all around the globe
  • Portfolio diversification
How does Projectopus work?

The Projectopus leverages the distinctive abilities and main interests of different types of project stakeholders to create a simple, logical, and extremely scalable business model.

How do I upload a concept?

First, you create a Provate account. If you’re an existing member you can login to your profile. After that you upload your concept and submit it so that a team of professionals can access it. Finally you’ll get updated by our team for further details.

How do I know if my concept is successfully uploaded ?

You’ll receive a confirmation email after successfully submitting your concept.

How long should i wait to recieve an email concerning the concept ?

Our specialized team will do their best to provide you with feedback within 8 to 15 working days after concept submit.

How do I know if the concept meets the requirements ?

You must follow the guideline.

How can I view recent concepts ?

You can read the reviews and feedback done by previous clients.

Can the project be edited after submission ?

After concept submission, no additional adjustments can be made.

If the terms were breached, what happens ?

The legal entity will be taking the needed actions as per the relevant contract

After contract acceptance, what are the procedures ?

An official legal contract will be created in a meeting in the presence of renowned third-party advisors

Can the issuer make a counter offer ?

Yes, a counter offer can be made to reach a middle agreement.

How will adopters be protected ?

The issued contract will be monitored by The Projectopus legal team and Vistra to maintain all ethical standards.

How will payments be securely made ?

Payments will be made through Vistra.
